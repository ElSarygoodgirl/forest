What is a tree?

We know trees are made of wood and have a trunk, branches and leaves, but how do we define what makes a tree… a tree? A tree is a tall plant that can live a very long time. It has a single stem or trunk and branches that support leaves.  Beneath the ground, a tree has a root system that acts as an anchor and stores the water and nutrients the plant needs to grow.
